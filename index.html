Welcome to Ozut! game rules.

Here you will find everything you need to understand the game.

Let's Start !

First step :
Choose the level

There is 5 different level, easy, intermediate, hard, nightmare and custom

Easy

  • For the noobs
  • Circles are big
  • Apparition circles and lines are ON
  • Top score disabled

Intermediate

  • Good for training
  • Circles have a medium size
  • Apparition circles and lines are ON
  • Top score disabled

Hard

  • For a little challenge
  • Circles have a small size
  • Apparition circles are ON, Apparition lines are OFF
  • Top score enabled

Nightmare

  • Now we can talk
  • Circles are tiny
  • Apparition circles and lines are OFF
  • Top score enabled

Custom

  • Play as you want
  • Top score disabled

Second Step :
How to play

The game duration will depend on the music you choose, expect on custom mod.

Once the game started, you must do the best score possible.

To do so, you'll have to click on the circles that appear on the screen

You can click with the following keys : 'E' and 'Z' or the left click

Be carefull, depending on the level, you cannot use all of them.

In nightmare level, 'Z' is the only key to press on the left and 'E' on the right

Third Step :
Endgame

The game can ends from two ways :

End of timer

You did it until the end !

Your score will be printed.

If you play in hard or nightmare, you can beat the top score.

Low accuracy

You lost before the end !

Your score will not be printed.

Try again if you want to beat the top score.
